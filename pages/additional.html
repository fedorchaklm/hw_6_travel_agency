EGYPT

We invite you on an exciting journey to the land of ancient pharaohs and majestic pyramids - Egypt! This country, located on the shores of the Red Sea and the Mediterranean Sea, impresses with its history, culture and sights.

Begin your journey with a visit to Cairo, the capital of Egypt, where you can see the world-famous Egyptian Museum with a unique collection of artifacts and treasures from ancient Egypt, including pharaonic masks and sarcophagi. See with your own eyes the Great Pyramid of Giza, one of the Seven Wonders of the World, and the mysterious Sphinx.

Next, we will go to Luxor, a city famous for its ancient temples and tombs of pharaohs. Visit the Temple of Karnak, impressive with its columns and statues, and the Temple of Hatshepsut, one of the most beautiful temples in Egypt.

Do not miss the opportunity to visit the most famous resort city of Egypt - Sharm el-Sheikh. Relax on the snow-white beaches of the Red Sea, enjoy incredible views of the underwater world while snorkeling and diving. Egypt awaits you with its rich history, incredible culture and the hospitality of its people.
